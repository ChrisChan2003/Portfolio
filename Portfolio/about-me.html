About Chris

“I have hated words and I have loved them, and I hope I have made them right.”

That is a quote from my favorite novel, The Book Thief by Markus Zusak. When I first read it with heavy eyelids in a dimly lit high school English classroom, I had no idea how much these sentences would resonate with me. Now, as an aspiring technical writer and recent graduate from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, I understand that words are much more than symbols on a page. They have genuine power, the ability to influence reality. I've dedicated my career to studying and harnessing that power.

Technology & Communication

My background in English, combined with interests in technology and psychology, makes me uniquely suited for technical communication. I approach writing with this mindset: making complex information clear, meaningful, and accessible. In other words, my goal is to make words “right" and give a voice to the audience.

I like challenging myself and learning new software. To me, overcoming the learning curves that come with picking up new technology is exciting. Whether I’m working with documentation platforms like MadCap Flare, programming in VSCode with HTML/CSS, or exploring accessibility and UX writing principles, I’m motivated by the challenge of turning complex ideas into user-friendly experiences.

Beyond the Profession

Outside of work, I’m passionate about video games and creative projects. I love immersing myself in the challenge, creativity, and world of imagination! For this reason, I have a soft spot for Indie games with rich world-building like Hollow Knight, Rain World, and Hades. I’m always open for talking about my interests, so please reach out!
